Official Report by UNFCCC summarizing the achievements of CDM





The document below is the official report by United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC) to summarize the achievements of Clean Development Mechanism (CDM) from 2001 through 2018.

Our project is shown on the cover page and page 15 of the PDF document. This clearly demonstrated the recognition of our solar cooker project by the United Nations due to its significant environmental and social benefits.

Document Title:

Achievements of the Clean Development Mechanism, Harnessing Incentive for Climate Action (2001-2018)

United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC), August 2018
